Mission

From my very first week on the floor as a nurse I realized that much of the technology we use as healthcare providers is not user friendly. Honestly, to many of us, it didn’t seem friendly to anyone. Myself and other staff would often become frustrated at how hard it seemed to use many of the items we needed every day. Electronic health records never seemed to really have the content we needed, intravenous pumps never seemed quite as smart as they were meant to be, many of the bedside products needed to be quality checked at inopportune times and some of them never seemed to load correctly. I immediately began dreaming of a better tomorrow for healthcare with improved technology. I aim to create technology for healthcare environments that is able to find the balance between the needs of the stakeholders – corporations, healthcare providers, and patients.
